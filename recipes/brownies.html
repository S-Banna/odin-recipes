Brownies Recipe

Description

Quick and easy tasty brownie recipe, with basic home ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sugar: 2 cups white sugar
  • Flour:: All purpose Flour for structure in the batter, 1 and a half cups
  • Butter:: one cup melted butter for richness and moisture
  • 4 Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder: half cup for the chocolate taste
  • Vanilla: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract for flavor
  • Half teaspoon Baking Powder
  • Half teaspoon Salt
  • Walnuts: optional, but they do provide a sweet crunch. Half cup of walnut halves is enough
